## Summary

This is the end of the "boring work suit." Our Women's Work Suit in Sharkskin is crafted from a unique sharkskin wool, a fabric whose subtle two-tone luster adds a layer of intelligence and sophistication to your professional look. It's the secret to standing out with quiet confidence.

Your New Secret Weapon for the Office This is the suit for the woman who understands that true style is in the details. It's the answer to the generic, forgettable work uniform. We've taken the classic, single-breasted power suit and crafted it from Sharkskin—a legendary fabric that signals a sophisticated, intelligent approach to professional dressing. The Subtle Power of Sharkskin Wool This suit's unique character comes from its exceptional fabric, chosen for its subtle visual texture and all-day performance. A Unique Two-Tone Luster: We use a Super 120s' worsted wool woven in a "sharkskin" pattern. This technique uses two different colored yarns to create a fabric with a slight, two-tone sheen that has more depth and character than a standard flat wool. Lightweight & Breathable: This fabric is lightweight and breathable, making it comfortable for year-round wear and long days at the office. A Palette of 7 Modern Colors: Choose from a curated selection of 7 professional shades, including a sharp petrol blue and a classic slate grey. Engineered for a Commanding Silhouette This suit is architected with clean, modern lines to create a look of pure, understated power.
