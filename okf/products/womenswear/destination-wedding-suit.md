## Questions & Answers

**Q:** Why is Fresco wool specifically good for a destination or outdoor wedding?  
**A:** Fresco wool is a high-twist, open-weave cloth. The open weave allows air to circulate through the fabric rather than trapping heat against the body, which is the defining problem with standard wool at an outdoor wedding in warm weather. It also has natural wrinkle resistance because the high twist of the yarns helps the fabric spring back to shape. For a suit that needs to travel, stay pressed through hours outdoors, and look correct in photographs taken at any point in the day, Fresco wool is the correct fabric choice in a way that most alternatives are not.

**Q:** Can the tunic and cape be different colors?  
**A:** Yes. The tunic and trousers are one fabric selection, and the cape is a separate selection. Both come in the same 13 Fresco wool colors. You can order the full ensemble in black for a unified look, or choose a contrasting combination. The satin silk lining of the cape is a third independent selection from 20 colors, visible when the cape moves. The price is the same regardless of color combination.

**Q:** How does the cape attach and stay in place?  
**A:** The cape is secured at the chest by a hand-made silver metallic brooch and chain fastening. It is not attached to the tunic itself, so it moves independently. The floor-length pleated back gives it weight and drape so it falls cleanly rather than floating or moving unpredictably. In movement, it sweeps naturally. In photographs, especially at outdoor venues with any wind, it creates a significant visual effect.
