## Questions & Answers

**Q:** Is this a costume or an actual tailored suit?  
**A:** It is a tailored suit, canvas front construction, 100% Melton wool, hand-finished buttonholes, and fully lined throughout. It happens to be designed as a screen-accurate replica of the Arthur Fleck Joker suit from 2019, but the construction is the same as a bespoke suit you would wear to a formal event. Many of our customers wear it to conventions, film premieres, editorial shoots, and themed events well beyond its first outing.

**Q:** How accurate is the burgundy red color to the film?  
**A:** The default color is burgundy red Melton wool, which is the closest available fabric to the suit as it appears in the film's climactic scenes. Melton wool reads as a deep, complex red with slight warmth, accurate to how the suit looks on screen rather than the slightly oversaturated way it sometimes appears in promotional stills. The gold yellow waistcoat is also the default, matching the three-piece combination Arthur Fleck wears on the stairs.

**Q:** Can I change the color or fabric if I want a different version of the suit?  
**A:** Yes. The product page lets you select from the full range of Melton wool colors for both the jacket and trousers, and separately for the waistcoat. The price remains the same regardless of your color selection. Some customers use this to build their own interpretation rather than a direct screen replica.

**Q:** What does the free test suit process involve?  
**A:** You enter your measurements on the product page when you order. We make a complete three-piece test suit in a neutral light fabric to those measurements and ship it to you. You try it on, identify any fit adjustments you want, chest, shoulders, trouser break, sleeve length, whatever, and send us your notes. We apply every adjustment to your final burgundy Joker suit. The test suit is yours to keep. The process takes longer than a standard order but results in a final suit that fits correctly.

**Q:** Is the jacket two-button or three-button?  
**A:** Two-button, single-breasted, matching the screen-accurate design. The sleeves have three-button vented cuffs, which sometimes causes confusion, but the jacket front has two buttons.

**Q:** How long does production and delivery take?  
**A:** The full process, initial test suit, your feedback, and production of the final suit, typically takes 6 to 8 weeks. We will confirm the current production timeline when you order. If you have a specific event date, contact us before ordering so we can confirm whether the timeline works.

**Q:** Do you ship internationally?  
**A:** Yes, both the test suit and your final Joker suit ship worldwide via trackable courier. Delivery times vary by destination.

**Q:** Can I order just the jacket or just the waistcoat separately?  
**A:** This listing is for the full three-piece, jacket, waistcoat, and trousers. If you are interested in individual pieces, contact us before ordering and we can discuss what is possible.

**Q:** How do I care for the suit?  
**A:** Dry clean only. Melton wool is a dense, tightly woven fabric that holds its shape well. It does not need frequent cleaning. Hang it on a proper suit hanger after each wear and allow it to air out. Spot clean where possible between dry cleans.
