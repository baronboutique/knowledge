## Summary

Our silk Nehru jacket is a classic. James Bond Nehru jacket designed for the stylish gentleman. A classic cut with a wonderful nubby silk fabric fits like a glove. It's also perfect for informal occasions like casual weddings and weekend parties. Silk Nehru jackets like the ones in James Bond are an iconic staple from the 1960s. The Dr. No silk Nehru jacket fits over your shirt and is cut to be slim fitting. Its iconic look is designed with a mandarin collar and ventless back. Baron Boutique takes this classic design and turns into a slim-fitting look that can be worn on any occasion.

The Nehru jacket is an artifact of quiet, global sophistication, a garment that exists outside the conventional boundaries of Western tailoring. Our interpretation is a direct homage to the iconic jacket from the film Dr. No, a piece that has come to define a certain kind of villainous, intellectual elegance. Form & Fabric The soul of the garment resides in its unique Noil silk cloth, a fabric prized for its characterful, nubby texture and its sophisticated, low lustre. The jacket's minimalist authority is established by its mandarin collar and clean, five-button front. The construction is meticulous, built upon a full, hand-basted canvas front and finished with hand-carved horn buttons. It forms the essential structure over precisely [tailored trousers](https://baronboutique.com/menswear/mens-pants).
