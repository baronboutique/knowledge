## Summary

The slim fit flannel dress pants are exceptionally high quality. Meticulously tailored wool flannel dress pants using traditional and modern sewing techniques. Handmade buttonholes to hand hemmed leg openings and genuine hand carved horn buttons for added elegance and uniqueness in this flannel dress pants. The lightweight gauge of wool flannel fabric offers ease of movement and all-day comfort and flexibility for all seasons.

Wool flannel is the connoisseur's choice for a trouser that bridges the gap between formal and casual with unparalleled elegance. This interpretation is commissioned in a lightweight, 10-ounce Super 120s' flannel, a cloth with a soft, brushed hand and a distinctly supple drape. It offers the visual depth of traditional flannel without the weight, making it a versatile staple for all but the warmest months. Form & Foundation The design of these flannel dress pants is an exercise in clean lines and subtle, functional detail. A grip-enforced waistband interior ensures a shirt remains perfectly tucked, while buckle side-adjusters provide a precise fit, eliminating the need for a belt. The trousers are lined to the knee, a classic tailoring detail that enhances comfort and allows the flannel to drape flawlessly. A Personal Commission The creation of your trousers is a collaborative process. You will define the final silhouette—from a sharp, slim fit to a more classic interpretation—and specify the details that make the garment uniquely yours. Accommodations, such as a fully extended lining, are naturally part of our bespoke service.
