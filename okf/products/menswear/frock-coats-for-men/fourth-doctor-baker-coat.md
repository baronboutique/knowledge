A timeless fourth Doctor Baker Horror Of Fang Baker coat is truly an iconic coat essential for your cosplay collection. The Fang Rock coat will not only look amazing but will also stand the test of time. Made from Melton wool this fourth Doctor coat will get you plenty of wear for many years.

Commissioned in a dense Melton wool, this garment is a direct recreation of the formidable greatcoat from The Horror of Fang Rock. It is an artifact of the Fourth Doctor's tenure, built not as a costume, but as a functional piece of outerwear with a specific, atmospheric provenance.
