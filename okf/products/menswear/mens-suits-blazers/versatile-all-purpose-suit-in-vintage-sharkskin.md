## Summary

Men's vintage sharkskin suit in slim fit offers a nice drape with subtle sheen appropriate for work and business environments. An example of confident tailoring this vintage sharkskin suit offers the utmost formal look ideal for summer weddings as well, making it a versatile all-purpose suit.

Sharkskin is a connoisseur's cloth, a two-toned worsted wool with a rich sartorial heritage rooted in the mid-century. It is prized for its uniquely smooth hand, its subtle, two-toned lustre, and its exceptional visual texture. To wear a sharkskin suit is to make a statement of sophisticated, vintage-inspired style. The Architecture of the Vintage Sharkskin Suit Our interpretation is commissioned in a fine Super 120s' worsted wool. The jacket is built upon a full, hand-basted canvas front, an unseen architecture that allows the garment to mold to the wearer over time. The trousers are designed with a full skirt-lining in the waistband for a clean, comfortable drape. Every detail, from the hand-carved horn buttons to the final silhouette you define, is executed with precision. A Personal Commission The creation of your suit is a collaborative process. Through our customizer, you will define the final silhouette—from a classic tailored fit to a more relaxed interpretation—and specify personal details, such as the inclusion of a ticket pocket. Accommodations, such as a fully extended trouser lining, are naturally part of our bespoke service.
