## Summary

The definitive grey pinstripe suit, an artifact of timeless sartorial authority. Tailored from a fine, lightweight Merino wool, it is an ideal choice for warm-weather business and travel. The classic two-button construction and sharp pinstripe create a look of enduring, intelligent style, with customizable details to match your personal expression.

The pinstripe suit is an artifact of pure sartorial authority, a foundational garment with a rich heritage in finance and halls of power. Our interpretation is commissioned in a fine, 9-ounce Super 150s' Merino wool, a cloth chosen for its crisp hand and the sharp, graphic clarity of its stripe. The Architecture of the Grey Pinstripe Suit The jacket is built upon a full, hand-basted canvas front, an unseen architecture that allows the garment to mold to the wearer over time. The trousers are designed with a full skirt-lining in the waistband for a clean, comfortable drape. Every detail, from the hand-carved horn buttons to the final silhouette you define, is executed with precision. It is a versatile canvas that can be tailored with classic details, such as double pleats, to evoke the golden age of Hollywood style. A Personal Commission The creation of your suit is a collaborative process. Through our customizer, you will define the final silhouette—from a classic tailored fit to a more relaxed interpretation—and specify personal details, such as the inclusion of a ticket pocket. Accommodations, such as a fully extended trouser lining, are naturally part of our bespoke service.
