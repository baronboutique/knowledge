## Summary

Every man should have wool flannel suits in his wardrobe. Start with a simple solid navy flannel suit with a perfect sleeve-length jacket and notch lapel front. Wool flannel suits are fashionable and comfortable for all seasons. Crafted from 100% flannel cloth and custom-made to order, the navy flannel suit is sure to become a favorite.

The navy flannel suit is a cornerstone of the sophisticated masculine wardrobe, a versatile garment that bridges the gap between formal and casual with unparalleled elegance. Our interpretation is commissioned in a fine Super 120s' wool flannel—a cloth with a soft, brushed hand and a distinctly supple drape, offering the visual depth of traditional flannel without the weight. The Architecture of the Navy Flannel Suit The jacket is built upon a full, hand-basted canvas front, an unseen architecture that allows the garment to mold to the wearer over time. The trousers are designed with a full skirt-lining in the waistband for a clean, comfortable drape. Every detail, from the hand-carved horn buttons to the final silhouette you define, is executed with precision. A Personal Commission The creation of your suit is a collaborative process. Through our customizer, you will define the final silhouette—from a classic tailored fit to a more relaxed interpretation—and specify personal details, such as the inclusion of a ticket pocket. Accommodations, such as a fully extended trouser lining, are naturally part of our bespoke service.
