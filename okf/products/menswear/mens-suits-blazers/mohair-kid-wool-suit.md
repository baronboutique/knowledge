## Summary

The mohair and wool suit is engineered for performance. The unique, spring-like structure of mohair fibers lends the cloth an exceptional crease resistance, ensuring you remain impeccably sharp from a transatlantic flight to an evening cocktail reception. Its natural wicking properties provide comfort in any climate, making it the definitive choice for the international business traveler. While its classic form anchors a formal ensemble, the jacket can also be commissioned with an unstructured construction, creating a versatile blazer that pairs with equal authority over fine chinos or linen trousers.

The mohair suit is an artifact of quiet, sophisticated power, with a rich heritage in British cinematic style. Our interpretation is commissioned in a fine Super 140s' blend of merino wool and mohair—a cloth prized for its remarkable crease resistance and its elegant, low lustre, making it the definitive choice for travel and long days where an impeccable appearance is non-negotiable.
