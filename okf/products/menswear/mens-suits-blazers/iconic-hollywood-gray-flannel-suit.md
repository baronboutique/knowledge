## Summary

A grey flannel suit is the outfit of preference and symbol of loyalty. You’ll definitely make a statement in this look. For men who are style-conscious, this iconic Hollywood grey flannel suit is the right choice. Customize your flannel suit to a style that spans years.

The grey flannel suit is an artifact of quiet, masculine authority. It is the definitive uniform of the mid-century executive, a garment that communicates an assured, intellectual confidence. Our interpretation honors this heritage, commissioning the suit in a fine Super 120s' wool flannel—a cloth with a soft, brushed hand and a distinctly supple drape. The Architecture of the Grey Flannel Suit The jacket is built upon a full, hand-basted canvas front, an unseen architecture that allows the garment to mold to the wearer over time. The two-button stance and classic side vents create a timeless silhouette. The trousers are designed with a full skirt-lining in the waistband for a clean, comfortable drape. Every detail, from the hand-carved horn buttons to the final silhouette you define, is executed with precision. A Personal Commission The creation of your suit is a collaborative process. Through our customizer, you will define the final silhouette—from a classic tailored fit to a more relaxed interpretation—and specify personal details, such as the inclusion of a ticket pocket. Accommodations, such as a fully extended trouser lining, are naturally part of our bespoke service.
