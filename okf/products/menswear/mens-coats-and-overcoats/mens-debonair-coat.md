## Summary

Men's debonair coat has plenty to offer to its wearer, from cleanly tailored lines to undeniably sophisticated glamour. This is the coat full of great possibility, designed with your warmth and comfort as the priority.

This is the definitive camel coat, reimagined in a robust, 18-ounce Melton wool. It is an artifact of understated luxury, a cornerstone of a well-considered wardrobe. Form & Substance The soul of the coat is its dense Melton wool, a fabric that is felted and heavily brushed to an exceptionally soft hand. A tailored point collar offers a sharper, more modern line than a traditional lapel. The coat's structure is built upon a hand-basted canvas front, a bespoke foundation ensuring a perfect drape over a suit or fine knitwear. The Bespoke Promise: Your Fitting Canvas A garment of this quality demands a perfect foundation. Your commission begins with a preliminary fitting coat—a toile—crafted to your initial measurements. This critical step allows for the meticulous refinement of the coat’s drape and silhouette, ensuring the final Melton wool piece is nothing short of perfect. The fitting canvas is, naturally, yours to retain. The delivery of both pieces is, of course, included as part of our service. Dry-clean.
