## Summary

Baron Boutique's flannel is an amazing fabric, lighter than traditional flannel with a nice soft hand. Grey flannel trousers handcrafted with superb quality and fit perfectly to any shape or size. Grey flannel trousers go well with just about any color shirt. Great casual or formal wear for weekdays or weekends and doesn't show the dirt, making the trousers a great choice for two or three days wear when away from laundry facilities.

The grey flannel trouser is the single most versatile and essential trouser in a gentleman’s wardrobe. It possesses a unique sartorial alchemy, pairing with nearly anything, from a formal [navy blazer](https://baronboutique.com/essential-navy-blazer) to a simple [fine cotton shirt](https://baronboutique.com/mens-white-egyptian-cotton-shirt).
