## Summary

Get in the men's soft linen-cotton blend shirts for that cool, comfortable feeling. Designed in half sleeves to complement and match any wardrobe in a neutral shade of black, white, navy, khaki, and more. The cotton and linen blend is easy to wash and care for. This linen and cotton blend absorbs moisture to make you feel much more comfortable in hot weather.

The linen and cotton shirt is an exercise in sartorial engineering. It combines the cool, breathable character of pure linen with the smooth, structured discipline of fine cotton. The result is a cloth that offers the best of both worlds: the nonchalant elegance of linen, with a superior resistance to creasing. The Foundation of Men's Linen Cotton Shirts The signature button-down collar provides a soft, structural roll—a detail of considered, relaxed elegance. It is a garment designed for versatility, pairing with tailored [office wear](https://baronboutique.com/menswear/mens-suits-blazers) with the same ease as it does with weekend attire. A Personal Commission The creation of your shirt is a collaborative process. Through our customizer, you will define its final character—from the precise fit and collar style to the choice of long or short sleeves. Each detail is an opportunity for personal expression. The Bespoke Promise: A Flawless Foundation A garment of such functional elegance demands an unimpeachable fit. Your commission therefore begins with a preliminary fitting shirt—a toile—crafted to your initial specifications.
