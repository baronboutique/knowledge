It is crafted from the shorter fibers of the silk cocoon, resulting in a fabric with a characterful, irregular surface that is both visually intriguing and exceptionally soft to the touch. This shirt is a definitive statement of relaxed, textural luxury. The Foundation of Men's Raw silk shirts The shirt's unique character resides in the inherent quality of its cloth.
