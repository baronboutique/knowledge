## Summary

The definitive third piece, this waistcoat is tailored from a fine Super 160s' worsted wool by Vitale Barberis Canonico. Its versatile design is engineered to complete a formal suit or to bring a touch of sartorial structure to a more casual ensemble. An essential garment of impeccable quality and timeless style.

The waistcoat, the essential third piece of a gentleman's ensemble, is elevated when commissioned in exceptional cloth. Our interpretation is rendered in a fine Super 160s’ worsted wool from the historic Vitale Barberis Canonico mill—a fabric prized for its luminous quality and impeccably smooth hand. The Foundation of a Mens Wool Dress Vest This garment is an exercise in versatility. It is designed to complete a formal suit, to be worn over a crisp shirt and fine [dress pants](https://baronboutique.com/mens-pure-new-wool-wedding-pants), or to lend a touch of tailored structure to a more casual pairing. The construction is meticulous, featuring hand-carved horn buttons and a silk back with a traditional adjuster for a precise fit. A Personal Commission The creation of your waistcoat is a collaborative process. The design can be rendered in its classic, lapel-less form, or commissioned with the addition of a collar and lapels for a more formal, vintage-inspired expression. Each detail is an opportunity for personal expression. The Bespoke Promise: Your Fitting Canvas A garment designed to sit with such precision against the torso demands an unimpeachable fit.
