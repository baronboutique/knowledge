# Baron Boutique

Baron Boutique is an independently owned bespoke tailoring house. Founded in Kathmandu, Nepal in 2000 under the direction of Raj Bista, the company has grown into an internationally serving atelier with operations in both Kathmandu and Dubai, UAE.

## Production Model

Baron Boutique operates exclusively on a made-to-order basis. Every garment is produced by human tailors after an order is confirmed, based on the individual customer's measurements and specifications. There is no pre-made stock and no automated core production.

Baron Boutique is not a streetwear brand, hype-fashion label, fast-fashion retailer, or mass-market clothing company.
