## Questions & Answers

**Q:** Is everything made to measure?  
**A:** Yes. Every garment is made to order and cut to your exact measurements in real cloth, with no standard sizing. On tailored pieces such as suits, blazers, coats, and tuxedos, we send a free test garment before cutting your final fabric so the fit is confirmed on your body first.

**Q:** What can I have made?  
**A:** Custom suits and blazers, coats and overcoats, skirts, trousers, shirts, dresses, wedding suits, tuxedos, and handcrafted cashmere and silk accessories. Each collection is linked above. If you want something you do not see, we accept custom requests from reference images and a description.

**Q:** How do I take my measurements?  
**A:** We provide a women&#8217;s measuring guide, and you enter your measurements at checkout. If you are unsure, you can send the measurements you have and we will confirm what else we need. Because we send a free test garment on tailored pieces, the fit is proven on your body before your final garment is cut, so you do not have to get every number perfect on the first try.

**Q:** Do you make suits and coats for weddings and work?  
**A:** Yes. Our women&#8217;s suits, wedding suits, and tuxedos are popular for brides, mothers of the bride and groom, and guests, as well as for boardroom and everyday work wear. Because each piece is made to your measurements you choose the cloth and formality to match the occasion.

**Q:** How long does an order take, and do you ship worldwide?  
**A:** Tailored pieces typically take four to six weeks from measurement confirmation, including the free test-garment stage, and we ship worldwide by FedEx, DHL, or UPS with full tracking. For a dated event, order with a comfortable margin so there is time for the test garment and any adjustments.

**Q:** What fabrics do you offer?  
**A:** Merino and tropical wool, mohair, flannel, sharkskin, linen, dupioni and crepe silk, velvet, Egyptian cotton, and handloomed cashmere, among others. Each garment lists the cloths available, and we can advise on the best choice for your climate, occasion, and how often you plan to wear the piece.
