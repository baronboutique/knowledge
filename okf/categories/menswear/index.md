Everything Here Is Cut to Your Body, Then Confirmed Before a Stitch Is Final
Baron Boutique makes menswear the way it was made before standard sizing existed: to your measurements, not to a size block. A suit, a coat, a shirt, or a tuxedo bought off the rack is fitted to an average of thousands of men, which is to say it is fitted to no one. The shoulders sit wrong, the waist is close-enough, and the sleeves are whatever the size block decided. We start from your measurements instead, and on the tailored pieces we send a free test garment in a low-cost cloth so the fit is confirmed on your body before we cut your final fabric.

Where to start
- Suits & Blazers: The core of the wardrobe, made to your measurements: silk suits in dupioni and raw silk, pinstripe and chalk-stripe suits, VBC and tropical wool, mohair, and soft unstructured suits.
- Wedding Suits: Baron’s signature territory, including the wedding suit with a cape that customers search for by name, plus linen grooms’ suits and destination-wedding tailoring.
- Tuxedos & Dinner Jackets: Black-tie done correctly and owned rather than rented: peak, shawl, and notch tuxedos, plus velvet and patterned dinner jackets for black tie with a point of view. Made to your measurements with a free test suit first.
- Coats, Overcoats & Frock Coats: Statement outerwear made to your measurements: overcoats and topcoats, peacoats and car coats, and the dramatic frock coats Baron is known for, from Victorian and gothic silhouettes to screen-inspired coats.
- Shirts & Trousers: Dress shirts in 220-thread Egyptian cotton, raw silk, and satin silk, cut to your neck and body rather than a collar size, and tailored trousers in silk, high-twist wool, and pinstripe.
- Screen-Inspired & Cosplay: Collector-grade replicas built as wearable tailoring, not costume: Doctor Who coats, The Matrix, James Bond, the North by Northwest suit, and more, all made to your measurements in real cloth.
- Fabric Collections: Velvet, Silk & Cashmere: Browse by cloth: velvet dinner jackets, smoking jackets, and statement coats; genuine dupioni, raw, and satin silk suits, shirts, and accessories; and 100% Grade A handwoven Himalayan cashmere.
- Vests & Accessories: The finishing pieces: tailored waistcoats, hand-finished satin silk ties and pocket squares, aviator silk scarves, and the men’s cashmere scarves and pashmina shawl. The easiest way into Baron craftsmanship, from $65.

## Questions & Answers

**Q:** What does Baron Boutique make?  
**A:** Baron makes made-to-your-measurements menswear across the whole wardrobe: suits and blazers, wedding suits, tuxedos and dinner jackets, coats, overcoats and frock coats, dress shirts, trousers, and waistcoats, plus a large catalog of screen-inspired and cosplay tailoring drawn from film and television. Alongside the tailoring are the fabric collections, velvet, genuine silk, and handwoven Himalayan cashmere, and finishing accessories including silk ties, pocket squares, and cashmere scarves. Everything is made by one workshop to a single standard.

**Q:** Is everything made to my measurements?  
**A:** The tailored garments, suits, jackets, coats, shirts, trousers, tuxedos, and waistcoats, are all made to your measurements with no standard sizes. Handwoven cashmere and the satin silk accessories are made to standard proportions, with free customization of length or color available on many of them. If a piece is made to measure, the product page will take your measurements at checkout and your order will include a free test garment before the final cloth is cut.

**Q:** How does the free test garment work?  
**A:** When you order a tailored piece, you enter your measurements on the product page. Before we cut your final cloth, we build a complete test garment in a low-cost fabric to those measurements and ship it to you by trackable courier. You try it on at home, check the fit, and send us any adjustments. We update your pattern and only then cut your final garment. The test garment is yours to keep. This is the step that lets us guarantee fit for customers ordering from anywhere in the world.

**Q:** Do you make screen-inspired and cosplay pieces?  
**A:** Yes, and it is one of Baron&#8217;s specialties. The screen-inspired catalog includes Doctor Who coats and jackets, The Matrix, James Bond tailoring, the Cary Grant North by Northwest suit, and many more, all built as wearable, made-to-your-measurements tailoring in real cloth rather than costume-grade materials. These are collector-quality replicas you can wear to a convention, a themed wedding, or simply as a distinctive garment, and they come with the same free test garment as the rest of the range.

**Q:** Can I choose a custom fabric, color, or detail?  
**A:** Yes. Most pieces offer a range of cloths and colors on the product page, and you can specify details such as lapel style, vents, lining, and button configuration in the order notes. If you want something outside the listed options, a specific color, a particular cloth, or a design change, contact us before ordering and we will confirm what is possible. Personalization such as monogramming is also available on many cashmere pieces.

**Q:** Do I need a tailor to take my measurements?  
**A:** No. Every measurement field on the product pages includes instructions, and most customers complete them in about 15 minutes with a soft tape measure and a second person to help. Small errors are normal, which is exactly what the free test garment is for: nothing is cut in your final cloth until you have tried the test garment on and confirmed the fit. You do not need any prior experience to order.

**Q:** How far in advance should I order for a wedding or event?  
**A:** For a fixed date such as a wedding, order at least 8 to 10 weeks ahead. That allows comfortable time for the test garment to reach you, for any fit adjustments, and for your final garment to be made and shipped. Production is typically four to six weeks from the point your measurements are confirmed, including the test-garment stage. If your date is sooner, contact us before ordering and we will tell you honestly whether the timeline works.

**Q:** Do you ship worldwide, and how do I pay?  
**A:** Yes, we ship worldwide from Dubai by trackable courier, with delivery usually 3 to 7 business days once a garment is finished. Checkout is secure through PayPal, and you can pay with your PayPal balance or with any major credit or debit card through PayPal&#8217;s guest checkout, with no PayPal account required.

**Q:** How is Baron different from off-the-rack or a local bespoke tailor?  
**A:** Off-the-rack fits an average body and offers no fit guarantee before you buy. A local bespoke tailor makes to your measurements but typically costs several times more and requires you to visit in person. Baron sits between them: genuinely made to your measurements, with a free test garment that confirms the fit before the final cloth is cut, at a price far below traditional bespoke, and available to order from anywhere in the world.

**Q:** What is the price range?  
**A:** The range spans from $65 for a hand-finished silk pocket square through roughly $199 to $299 for cashmere scarves and shirts, $499 to $799 for most suits, tuxedos, and coats, and up to around $1,499 for the most elaborate pieces such as the cape wedding suit. Made-to-your-measurements construction and the free test garment are included on the tailored pieces at no extra charge.

**Q:** Can I return a made-to-measure garment?  
**A:** Because each tailored garment is cut specifically to your measurements, custom pieces are not eligible for return or refund once production has begun. This is exactly why the free test garment exists: fit concerns are identified and resolved on the test garment before your final cloth is cut, and any remaining fit issues are handled through our alteration service. Ready-made accessories follow standard return terms.

**Q:** Does Baron make womenswear too?  
**A:** Yes. Alongside this menswear range, Baron makes made-to-your-measurements womenswear including suits, tuxedos, coats, dresses, and separates, plus the shared cashmere collection. The same workshop, the same standard, and the same free-test-garment process apply. You can reach the women&#8217;s range from the main navigation.
