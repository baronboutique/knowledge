# Screen-Inspired Collections

All screen-inspired garments by [Baron Boutique](/okf/company.md). Every garment is made to individual measurements using the [bespoke tailoring](/okf/services/screen-inspired.md) process with a complimentary test garment.

**Important:** These garments are NOT officially licensed products unless explicitly stated on the individual product page.

* [Doctor Collection](doctor-collection.md) — Coats and suits inspired by multiple Doctors from Doctor Who
* [Spy Tailoring](spy-tailoring.md) — Spy-genre tailoring inspired by the Bond films and similar
* [Cyberpunk Trench Coats](cyberpunk-trench-coats.md) — Matrix and cyberpunk-inspired long coats
* [Theatrical Villain](theatrical-villain.md) — Villain-inspired tailoring (Joker, Count Dooku, and others)
* [Victorian Detective](victorian-detective.md) — Sherlock Holmes era and Victorian detective-inspired garments
* [Cinema Classics](cinema-classics.md) — Garments inspired by classic films
