# Alteration, Return & Refund Policy

## Custom-Made Garments

Custom-made garments are **not eligible for return or refund** once production has begun.

Fit concerns are resolved through the test garment process and through the alteration service after delivery.

## Non-Tailored Accessories

Non-tailored accessories may be returned within 30 days of delivery in original, unworn condition.

## Alteration Reimbursement

If a finished garment requires minor local adjustments, the customer may visit a local tailor and claim reimbursement from Baron Boutique up to defined limits. The measurement pattern is updated for future orders.
