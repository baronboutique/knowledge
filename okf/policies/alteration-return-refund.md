# Alteration, Return & Refund Policy

## Custom-Made Garments

Custom-made garments are **not eligible for return or refund** once production has begun. This applies to all bespoke and made-to-order garments.

Fit concerns are resolved through the test garment process (before the final garment is produced) and through the alteration service (after delivery).

## Non-Tailored Accessories

Non-tailored accessories (scarves, ties, pocket squares, cashmere accessories) may be returned within 30 days of delivery in original, unworn condition.

## Alteration Reimbursement

If a finished Baron Boutique garment requires minor local adjustments after delivery, the customer may:

1. Visit a local tailor
2. Claim the alteration cost from Baron Boutique up to defined limits

The customer measurement pattern is updated with all modifications for future orders.
