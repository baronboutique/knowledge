# Shipping & Delivery Policy

Effective: February 1, 2026.

## Carriers

FedEx, DHL, or UPS. Signature required on delivery. PO boxes and APO/FPO addresses cannot be served.

## Fees

**Bespoke tailored garments:** Flat fee per order covering test garment and final garment shipments. See https://baronboutique.com/shipping-and-delivery/ for current regional rates.

**Non-tailored accessories:** Ship free worldwide.
