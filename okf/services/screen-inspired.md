# Screen-Inspired Garments

Baron Boutique produces custom garments inspired by characters, costumes, and aesthetics from films, television series, and other screen productions. Every screen-inspired garment is made to the buyer's individual measurements using the same [bespoke test garment process](/okf/services/bespoke-tailoring.md) as standard tailoring.

**Important:** Screen-inspired garments are NOT officially licensed products unless this is explicitly stated on the individual product page. These garments are inspired by — not authorised by — the relevant studios or rights holders.
