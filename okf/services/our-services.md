# Our Services

**URL:** https://baronboutique.com/our-services/

## Questions & Answers

**Q:** Q. How does the online consultation process work for bespoke and made-to-measure services?  
**A:** A. Our online consultation involves a detailed discussion about your style preferences, garment needs, and fit requirements. We guide you through the measurement process with easy-to-follow instructions and visual aids to ensure accuracy from the comfort of your home. Submit the form with your required service to get started.

**Q:** Q. What kind of communication can I expect during the creation of my custom garment?  
**A:** A. We believe in keeping you informed every step of the way. You can expect regular updates on the progress of your garment, including photos or videos at key stages. Our team is also readily available to answer any questions you may have throughout the process via email.

**Q:** Q. If I'm unsure about the design, do you offer any design assistance?  
**A:** A. Yes, we provide design assistance. Baron offers personalized recommendations based on your needs and current trends.

**Q:** Q. What are the shipping options and estimated delivery times for international orders?  
**A:** A. We offer international shipping via established courier providers such as FedEx, DHL, and UPS. Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary depending on your location, courier operations, and customs processing. Tracking details are provided once your order is dispatched.

**Q:** Q. What happens if I need alterations after receiving my made-to-measure garment since I'm located internationally?  
**A:** A. For both bespoke and made-to-measure orders, we include a complimentary test fit to minimize the need for alterations. However, if minor adjustments are necessary—such as sleeve or pant length alterations—we recommend visiting a trusted local tailor. For more significant modifications, please contact us to receive shipping instructions for returning the garment to us for alteration.

**Q:** Q. Can I see examples of previous online custom projects you've completed?  
**A:** A. Yes, we have pictures of custom garments we've created for clients worldwide through our online services. Please contact us, and we'd be happy to share relevant examples of our craftsmanship.
