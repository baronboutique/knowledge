## Questions & Answers

**Q:** What is Private Label?  
**A:** Private label means we produce goods (in this case, custom tailored clothing or cashmere items) specifically for your brand, which you then sell under your own name. You control the branding, marketing, and pricing.

**Q:** What's the difference between Private Label and White Label?  
**A:** They are often used interchangeably. Technically, white label might refer to generic products that multiple brands can re-brand, while private label often implies products created exclusively for one specific brand, potentially with more customization. At Baron Boutique, we offer fully customized private label solutions tailored to your unique brand requirements.

**Q:** What specific products can you create under private label?  
**A:** We specialize in two main areas: Custom Tailoring (Suits, jackets, trousers, shirts, vests, coats - Bespoke &amp; MTM) and Cashmere &amp; Fine Knitwear (Scarves, shawls, stoles, sweaters, cardigans, robes, throws).

**Q:** Do you have Minimum Order Quantities (MOQs)?  
**A:** MOQs vary depending on the product type, complexity, and materials chosen. We strive to offer flexible solutions. Please outline your estimated quantities in the inquiry form for specific details.

**Q:** What is the typical process and timeline?  
**A:** The process includes consultation, design/material finalization, sampling, production, and delivery. Timelines depend on project scope, complexity, quantity, and material availability. We provide estimates after the initial consultation.

**Q:** Can I provide my own designs and specifications?  
**A:** Absolutely! We encourage you to bring your unique vision and work collaboratively based on your designs, tech packs, or detailed specifications.

**Q:** Can you help with sourcing materials?  
**A:** Yes, we leverage our extensive experience and relationships to source high-quality fabrics, cashmere yarns, and trims, or work with materials you specify.

**Q:** How is branding (labels, tags, packaging) handled?  
**A:** We integrate your supplied woven labels, care labels, hang tags, etc., into the finished products and can advise on placement. Packaging solutions can be discussed.

**Q:** How do I get started and receive a quote?  
**A:** Fill out the detailed Inquiry Form on this page. Provide as much information as possible, and our private label specialist will contact you to discuss next steps and work towards a tailored quote.

**Q:** What's the key difference between private label and white label products?  
**A:** Private label gives you control over the product itself, allowing for unique customization beyond just packaging, so your products can differ significantly from competitors using the same manufacturer. White label, on the other hand, involves branding pre-existing products, with customization typically limited to packaging and branding elements.

**Q:** What are your sampling costs?  
**A:** We charge for samples. Inventory samples are 2x wholesale cost; custom samples are 3x wholesale cost + operation fee for custom designs. This fee is credited back with your bulk order.
