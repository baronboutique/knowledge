# Alteration Service

Baron Boutique offers a garment alteration service. This service is not restricted to Baron-made garments — any garment may be submitted.

## Alteration Reimbursement

If a finished Baron Boutique garment requires minor local adjustments after delivery, the customer may visit a local tailor and claim alteration cost reimbursement from Baron Boutique up to defined limits. See [Returns & Alterations Policy](/okf/policies/returns-alterations.md) for details.
