## Questions & Answers

**Q:** Q. What makes Himalayan hemp different from other hemp fabric?  
**A:** A. Our hemp is cultivated at high altitudes in Nepal's pristine mountain environment, where clean air, natural rainfall, and chemical-free farming create superior fiber quality. Combined with traditional handweaving techniques passed down through generations, our Himalayan hemp offers exceptional strength, softness, and durability that mass-produced hemp cannot match.

**Q:** Q. Is hemp fabric really better than cotton or linen?  
**A:** A. Hemp uses 50% less water than cotton, requires no pesticides, and produces three times stronger fabric. Compared to linen, hemp is more durable, softer over time, and offers better UV protection. Unlike synthetic fabrics that shed microplastics, hemp is 100% biodegradable and becomes more comfortable with every wash.

**Q:** Q. Does hemp fabric feel rough or scratchy?  
**A:** A. Modern hemp fabric, especially our handwoven Himalayan hemp, has a soft, breathable texture similar to linen. What makes hemp remarkable is that it gets softer and more comfortable with every wash—unlike synthetic fabrics that deteriorate over time. Many customers describe well-worn hemp as feeling like their favorite broken-in t-shirt.

**Q:** Q. How do I care for 100% hemp clothing and fabric?  
**A:** A. Hemp is remarkably low-maintenance. Machine wash cold or hand wash with gentle detergent. Air dry or tumble dry low. Hemp actually softens and improves with washing. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals. The natural antimicrobial properties mean hemp requires less frequent washing than synthetic fabrics. We include detailed care instructions with every order.

**Q:** Q. Is your hemp sustainable and ethically sourced?  
**A:** A. Yes. Our hemp is grown without pesticides, herbicides, or synthetic fertilizers in Nepal's high-altitude regions. We work directly with artisan farming communities, ensuring fair compensation and preserving traditional cultivation methods. Hemp naturally enriches soil, requires minimal water, and the entire plant is utilized with zero waste. We use Azo-free natural dyes and provide full traceability from field to finished product.

**Q:** Q. Can I get hemp fabric in custom colors?  
**A:** A. Absolutely! We offer Pantone color matching and have over 350 hand-dyed colors available. Our artisans hand-dye hemp using eco-friendly, Azo-free dyes. Whether you need a specific brand color for your fashion line or a custom shade for personal garments, we can create it.

**Q:** Q. What's the minimum order for custom hemp fabric or private label?  
**A:** A. We support emerging designers and brands with flexible minimums. Hemp fabric by the meter can start at 10 meters. Private label hemp garments have a minimum of 10 pieces per style. For individual custom garments, there's no minimum—you can order a single bespoke hemp piece.

**Q:** Q. How long does hemp fabric last compared to synthetic materials?  
**A:** A. Hemp is one of the most durable natural fibers available—three times stronger than cotton. A well-made hemp garment can last 20+ years with proper care, while synthetic fabrics typically deteriorate within 2-5 years. Hemp's strength increases when wet, and the fabric actually improves over time. This makes hemp a true investment piece versus disposable fast fashion.

**Q:** Q. Is hemp clothing suitable for sensitive skin?  
**A:** A. Yes! Hemp is naturally hypoallergenic and antimicrobial. Our chemical-free cultivation and processing means no harsh pesticides, synthetic dyes, or chemical treatments touching your skin. Many customers with sensitivities to synthetic fabrics find hemp to be exceptionally comfortable and gentle.

**Q:** Q. Can you create custom hemp garments like suits, dresses, or specialty items?  
**A:** A. Yes. Baron Boutique offers full bespoke and made-to-measure services using 100% natural hemp fabric. From tailored suits and elegant dresses to custom scarves and specialty garments—our master tailors can create any design. We also offer CMT (Cut, Make, Trim) services if you have your own hemp designs.

**Q:** Q. Do you ship hemp fabric and clothing internationally?  
**A:** A. Yes, we ship 100% natural hemp products worldwide with tracked delivery included. International orders typically arrive within 1-2 weeks after production. We handle export documentation and ensure secure, protective packaging for safe transit.

**Q:** Q. What fabric weights and weaves do you offer?  
**A:** A. We offer hemp fabric in multiple weights ranging from lightweight (150gsm) for summer wear to heavyweight (400gsm) for outerwear and upholstery. Available weaves include plain weave, twill, and herringbone. Each weave and weight offers different characteristics for drape, durability, and texture. Contact us to discuss which option best suits your project.

**Q:** Q. Why should I choose 100% natural fiber like hemp in 2026?  
**A:** A. The 2026 consumer trend shows a dramatic shift toward "100% natural fiber" as people question synthetic materials. Searches for "is polyester bad," "100% cotton clothing," "100% linen," and "100% wool" have reached all-time highs. Hemp fits perfectly into this movement—it's stronger than cotton, more sustainable than linen, plant-based (vegan), and unlike polyester, it doesn't shed microplastics or deteriorate over time. Hemp represents the future of conscious, quality-driven fashion.

Contact us in advance to arrange a visit to our Nepal workshop where you can meet our artisans, see traditional hemp weaving techniques, and experience the quality of Himalayan hemp production.

**Q:** Q. What's the difference between your hemp and industrial hemp fabric?  
**A:** A. Industrial hemp fabric is typically mass-produced with mechanical processing that can compromise fiber quality and softness. Our Himalayan hemp is cultivated at high altitude, hand-processed using traditional methods, and handwoven by skilled artisans. This results in superior texture, durability, and a softer hand feel. The artisanal approach also ensures fair wages and preserves cultural heritage.
