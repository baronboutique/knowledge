# CMT (Cut, Make & Trim)

In a CMT arrangement, the customer supplies their own fabric and/or accessories. Baron Boutique provides the cutting, construction, and finishing of the garment.

CMT can be executed as either:
- [Bespoke](/okf/services/bespoke-tailoring.md) — with a cotton test garment before the final fabric is cut
- [Made-to-Measure](/okf/services/made-to-measure.md) — pattern from measurements, no test garment
