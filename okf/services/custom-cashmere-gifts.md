# Custom Cashmere Gifts

Each piece is handwoven in Nepal by artisan partners using the world’s finest cashmere. We offer fully personalized options, including Pantone-matched colors, custom monograms, delicate scent infusions, and eco-friendly luxury packaging, with flexible minimums starting at just 6 pieces per style.

## Questions & Answers

**Q:** How do I get started with my custom cashmere gift order?  
**A:** Click any "Plan Your Custom Gifts" or consultation button on this page to fill out our detailed inquiry form. Provide information about your occasion, desired products, colors, quantities, customization preferences, budget range, and event timeline. Our team responds within 24-48 hours to begin your personalized consultation and provide a tailored quote.

**Q:** What is the minimum order quantity for custom cashmere gifts?  
**A:** Our minimum order is just 6 pieces per style. This flexible MOQ makes luxury cashmere gifting accessible whether you're planning an intimate bridal party of six or a corporate event with 100+ attendees.

**Q:** How much do custom cashmere gifts cost?  
**A:** Pricing varies based on product type (scarf vs. blanket), cashmere quality (100% pure vs. blends), quantity ordered, level of customization (colors, embroidery, packaging), and any special requests. After you share your requirements through our inquiry form, we'll provide a detailed, transparent quote tailored to your budget.

**Q:** Can I order samples before placing my full order?  
**A:** Yes! We provide either physical samples (available for a fee that's credited back toward your bulk order) or detailed digital mock-ups showing your custom colors, monograms, and packaging design. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the design before production begins.

**Q:** What are your sampling costs?  
**A:** Inventory samples (existing colors/styles) are 2x wholesale cost. Custom samples (unique colors, monograms, designs) are 3x wholesale cost plus an operation fee. All sampling fees are credited back when you place your bulk order, making it risk-free to ensure quality before committing.

**Q:** What payment terms and methods do you accept?  
**A:** We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For larger corporate or bulk orders, we can discuss flexible payment terms (such as deposit + balance upon completion). All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees—your quote includes production, customization, and worldwide shipping.

**Q:** What personalization and customization options do you offer?  
**A:** We offer extensive customization: Pantone-matched custom colors, embroidered monograms (initials, names, dates, logos), custom woven labels for private branding, scent infusions (rose, lavender, sandalwood, chamomile, vanilla, cedarwood), luxury gift packaging with silk ribbons and branded boxes, and coordinated accessory sets (scarves + gloves + shawls).

**Q:** Can you match my exact wedding colors or corporate brand palette?  
**A:** Absolutely! We offer Pantone color matching and have over 350 hand-dyed colors available. Simply share your Pantone codes, fabric swatches, color references, or even inspiration photos, and our artisans will hand-dye your cashmere to match perfectly.

**Q:** Can each gift in my order be personalized differently?  
**A:** Absolutely! Each piece can feature unique personalization—different colors, individual monograms, varied embroidery, or distinct names/initials. This is ideal for bridesmaids with different names, corporate gifts with employee initials, or baby shower gifts with each child's name and birthdate.

**Q:** Can I add corporate logos or complex embroidery designs?  
**A:** Yes! We handle everything from simple monograms and initials to detailed corporate logos and custom embroidery designs. Share your logo files, design specifications, or artwork, and we'll work with you to determine the best placement, size, thread colors, and embroidery technique for your cashmere items.

**Q:** What cashmere products can I order for gifting?  
**A:** We create cashmere scarves (various sizes and weights), shawls and wraps, stoles, baby blankets, throws, and coordinated accessory sets including gloves, hats, and booties. All items are available in 100% pure cashmere or premium blends like cashmere-silk and cashmere-cotton depending on your needs and budget.

**Q:** Can you create custom cashmere for occasions not listed on your page?  
**A:** Absolutely! While we highlight weddings, Mother's Day, corporate events, baby showers, and competition prizes, we create bespoke cashmere for any meaningful moment—graduations, retirements, milestone birthdays, religious ceremonies, memorial gifts, holiday gifting, or "just because." Share your vision and we'll bring it to life.

**Q:** What is the difference between a cashmere shawl and a cashmere wrap for gifting?  
**A:** A cashmere shawl is typically larger — designed to drape over the shoulders and cover the back — while a cashmere wrap is lighter and more versatile, worn loosely around the neck or shoulders. For gifting purposes, wraps are generally more practical as everyday pieces; shawls read as more ceremonial and are popular for weddings and formal occasions. Both are available to order from Baron Boutique in custom colors with monogramming and gift packaging.

**Q:** What are the best personalized cashmere gifts for a new baby?  
**A:** The best personalized cashmere gifts for a newborn are baby blankets and baby sweaters in ultra-soft cashmere-silk or cashmere-cotton blends, embroidered with the baby's name and birthdate. Cashmere is naturally hypoallergenic and temperature-regulating, making it more suitable for sensitive newborn skin than synthetic or standard wool alternatives. Baron Boutique makes both cashmere baby blankets and baby sweaters to order, using baby-safe Azo-free dyes and Grade A cashmere, with each piece packaged for gifting.

**Q:** What are some unique heirloom-quality baby gifts available to order online?  
**A:** Handwoven cashmere baby blankets and sweaters personalized with a child's name and birthdate are among the few baby gifts that function as true keepsakes — soft enough for daily use, durable enough to last decades, and distinctive enough to be kept rather than replaced. Baron Boutique makes both to order from Nepal, with monogramming, color choice, and gift packaging included. These are not stock items — each piece is made after the order is placed.

We use 100% pure Grade A cashmere from the Tibetan Plateau, hand-dye to your exact specifications, offer true bespoke customization, and maintain direct relationships with our artisans ensuring ethical production and exceptional quality control.

**Q:** Is your cashmere ethically sourced and sustainable?  
**A:** Yes. We work directly with skilled artisans in Nepal who handcraft every piece using 100% pure Grade A cashmere sustainably sourced from the Tibetan Plateau. We use Azo-free, eco-friendly dyes, prioritize fair artisan compensation, and ensure every step meets strict standards for craftsmanship and sustainability.

**Q:** What makes a cashmere gift genuinely luxurious versus just expensive?  
**A:** A genuinely luxurious cashmere gift is distinguished by three things: the grade of cashmere used, whether the piece is handwoven or machine-made, and the level of personalization. Grade A cashmere handwoven on traditional looms by artisans produces a texture and drape that machine-produced cashmere does not replicate, regardless of price point. Baron Boutique uses 100% pure Grade A cashmere from the Tibetan Plateau, handwoven in Nepal, and personalizes each piece to the buyer's specifications — which is what separates a bespoke cashmere gift from a branded retail product.

**Q:** Are personalized cashmere scarves worth buying as gifts?  
**A:** Personalized cashmere scarves are among the most practical luxury gifts because they are used regularly, last for decades with basic care, and carry a level of personalization that mass-produced gifts cannot replicate. A cashmere scarf monogrammed with someone's initials or dyed to their preferred color is a gift they will reach for every season rather than shelve. Baron Boutique personalizes each scarf with the recipient's chosen colors, monograms, and packaging — making it a complete gift rather than a product in a box.

**Q:** How do recipients care for their cashmere gifts?  
**A:** Each gift includes detailed care instructions. Generally: hand wash in cool water with gentle wool/cashmere shampoo or dry clean, lay flat to dry (never wring or hang), store folded in breathable cotton bags with cedar sachets to prevent moths, and avoid direct sunlight. Proper care ensures cashmere remains luxurious for years.

We can also add natural, gentle scent options like chamomile or vanilla. Each blanket is handcrafted with care specifically for sensitive baby skin.
